Application and practice of creating durable images

This project analyzes fuel price trends in the UK by integrating multiple datasets and building an end-to-end data pipeline with AWS S3, Snowflake, and Power BI. The process includes web scraping, data cleaning, storage, transformation, and visualization to uncover key insights. Key steps: - **Data Collection & Processing** – Merged fuel, oil, currency, and inflation data. - **Storage & Pipeline** – Stored in S3, processed in Snowflake, automated ingestion via Snowpipe. - **Exploratory Analysis** – 21 SQL queries, correlation & anomaly detection, impact of external events. - **Visualization** – Power BI dashboard, trend analysis, and deep insights. Next steps: **Predictive modeling (ML), interactive Plotly Dash visualizations, and deployment.**
